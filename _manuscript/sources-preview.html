Major Initiatives and Projects

Data and Analytics Platforms

  • Humanitarian Data Insights Platform (HDIP)
    • Launched: Public beta December 10, 2024
    • Partners: DataKind, Save the Children, Microsoft
    • Technology: Uses generative AI to streamline data accessibility and predict metadata
    • Source: https://www.datakind.org/2024/08/06/humanitarian-data-insights-project-using-generative-ai-to-streamline-data-access-and-use-for-humanitarian-action/
  • DISHA (Data Insights for Social and Humanitarian Action)
    • Organization: UN Global Pulse partnership
    • Technology: AI-powered platform using mobile phone, satellite, and digital transaction data
    • Application: Real-time displacement patterns and disaster damage tracking

Key Statistics and Findings

Workforce Trends

  • Tokyo University Study: 97% of students reported using GPT for coursework, indicating a new generation of aid workers familiar with AI tools

Applications

  • Advocacy and Communication: ChatGPT used for drafting social media posts, media releases, and articles
  • Data Analysis: AI enables analysis of vast datasets to predict trends and inform humanitarian response
  • Predictive Analytics: Shift from reactive to anticipatory humanitarian action
  • Legal Analysis: Bespoke generative AI instances to query refugee law and understand protection needs

Challenges and Risks

  • Data Privacy: ChatGPT privacy statement indicates staff read and record information to improve algorithms
    • Risk: Humanitarians must avoid inputting sensitive operational data or personally identifiable information
  • Algorithmic Bias: Poor data quality and biased algorithms can lead to unfair outcomes
  • Equity Concerns: Potential to exacerbate vulnerabilities and inequalities
